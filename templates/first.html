About Us

At EqualEdge, we are dedicated to making education accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or background. Our platform provides inclusive assessment tools and resources that empower teachers to create fair, disability-friendly assessments and support students in diverse learning environments. By prioritizing accessibility, adaptability, and equity, we aim to foster a culture of inclusion where every learner can thrive and every educator is equipped to succeed. Together, we're breaking barriers and building a future where education works for everyone.
